About Us

Shaping IT Professionals Since 1998 — Trusted by Families Across Kannauj.

Subhash Academy (College of IT & Management) is an AICTE approved institution affiliated to Makhanlal Chaturvedi National University, Bhopal. We have been delivering quality professional education to students of Chhibramau, Kannauj, Farrukhabad and surrounding regions for over two decades.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that prepares students for the challenges of the modern world, fostering critical thinking, innovation, and ethical leadership. We believe every student deserves access to excellent professional education regardless of their background.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution of academic excellence, producing graduates who are not only technically proficient but also socially responsible and ethically sound — future leaders who make a positive impact on society.

Est. 1998

Subhash Academy was founded in Chhibramau, Kannauj with a mission to bring reliable IT and management education to students of rural and semi-urban Uttar Pradesh.

AICTE Approved

Recognized and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (ID: 1-44512605107), ensuring programs meet the highest national standards for professional education.

MCU Affiliated

Affiliated to Makhanlal Chaturvedi National University of Journalism & Communication, Bhopal — a renowned institution known for academic excellence and industry-aligned curriculum.
